Portfolio Punch-Up Proposal

**What is the work I’m taking on?**

I will create a small interactive scene for my About page inspired by classic platform games.
Users control a character and jump to hit floating blocks. When a block is hit, a fun fact about me appears.

This is not a full game. It is a single screen interaction with simple controls and “fake physics” (fixed straight-up jump only).

**Scope:**

-   One screen
-   5–8 blocks
-   Keyboard jump interaction
-   Pop-up fact cards
    The goal is playful discovery rather than gameplay.

**How will this enhance my body of work?**

This adds a unique interactive element to my portfolio and makes my About page more engaging than static text.

It demonstrates:

-   interaction design thinking
-   front-end implementation skills
-   personality and storytelling
-   ability to turn information into an experience
-   Along with reading about me, visitors can also explore and discover.
